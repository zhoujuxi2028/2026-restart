## 💡 Pro Tips for Day 10

1. **Treat the Mock Interview Seriously**
   - Simulate real conditions (video call, quiet room, professional attire)
   - Don't pause or restart - push through difficult moments
   - Record everything for honest self-assessment

2. **Focus on Confidence, Not Perfection**
   - You won't know everything - that's okay
   - Practice saying "I'm not familiar with that specific tool, but here's my approach..."
   - Confidence comes from preparation, not memorization

3. **Prepare for the Unexpected**
   - They might ask about technologies not in the job description
   - Have a framework for tackling unknown topics
   - Example: "While I haven't used [X] directly, in similar scenarios I've..."

4. **Rest and Recharge**
   - Don't cram tonight before the interview
   - Get good sleep, exercise, eat well
   - Trust your 9 days of preparation

5. **Logistics Matter**
   - Test video/audio setup 1 hour before
   - Have backup internet (mobile hotspot)
   - Prepare your environment (lighting, background, notes)
