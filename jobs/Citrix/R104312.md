## 📝 职位描述

The Lead Software Development Engineer in Test will work closely with Citrix Product Development and Test teams to design and implement scalable, long‑term automation strategies, while also contributing to the development and enhancement of automation infrastructure and frameworks. This role directly supports faster and consistent delivery of high‑quality, enterprise‑grade software solutions.

---

## 🎯 职责 (Duties and Responsibilities)

- Design and implement flexible Automation testing solutions and test scripts for Citrix products
- Execute Automation tests and identify product and infrastructure problems for quality assurance, collaborate with product stakeholders to support the root cause analysis and addressing
- Participate in Automation platform and infrastructure related development and maintenance
- Interface with internal teams to understand requirement for Product Automation development and solutions delivery
- Understand Citrix products and technologies for Automation strategy and priority alignment
- Continuously learning to keep pace with technology advancements
- Create documentation of agreed upon designs and implementation details
- Take part in planning and daily standups to ensure Automation commitment

---

## ✅ 必备条件 (Required Experience/Skills)

- Bachelor or master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or Electrical Engineering
- **8+ years** experience of system level programming and test on Windows or Unix/Linux
- Proficiency in **C#, PowerShell, and Python**, or equivalent experience
- Familiarity with additional stacks: Java, Go, or front‑end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) is a plus
- Strong expertise in software testing methodologies and automation test cases design
- Ability to deploy automation setups including frameworks, approaches, and automation environments
- Experienced in testing frameworks: **Robot Framework, Selenium**
- Experienced in Automation tools: **Terraform, Ansible**, and continuous integration pipelines
- Strong **English communication skills** (written and verbal) - collaborates with global engineering teams
- In-depth knowledge of software, operating system, networking
- Skills to leverage **AI** in daily work
- Strong problem solving and analytical skills

---

## ⭐ 优先条件 (Preferred Qualifications)

- Experience with container-based software development (e.g. **Docker, Kubernetes**)
- Understanding of building systems and the complexities of packaging and shipping software
- Experience working with Cloud providers (ideally **Azure**)
- Good understanding of Windows System Management and Database fundamentals (**SQL**)
- Self-motivated and tenacious when tackling tough technical issues
- Good team player
